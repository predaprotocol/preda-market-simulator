## Core Features

### Simulation Engine
- Configurable market parameters (duration, participants, volatility, thresholds)
- Async/await support for concurrent simulations
- Seed-based reproducibility for deterministic results
- Real-time BSI (Belief State Index) evolution tracking

### Market Scenarios
- 9 predefined scenarios: BullishTrend, BearishTrend, Sideways, SentimentReversal,
  ConsensusFormation, HighVolatility, LowActivity, FlashCrash, ParabolicRise
- Custom scenario support
- Scenario-specific shock events (flash crashes, sentiment reversals)

### Participant Modeling
- 6 behavior types: Rational, Momentum, Contrarian, Random, Conservative, Aggressive
- Realistic trading decisions based on BSI and market conditions
- Configurable risk tolerance and capital allocation

### Oracle Simulation
- BSI generation with drift, noise, and mean reversion
- Target-based belief evolution
- Shock application for stress testing
- Configurable update frequency and noise levels

### Trading Strategies
- 4 built-in strategies: ThresholdCrossing, Momentum, MeanReversion, Contrarian
- Strategy backtesting framework
- Performance metrics: Sharpe ratio, win rate, max drawdown, total return

### Analytics
- Comprehensive performance metrics
- Scenario comparison capabilities
- Market statistics tracking
- Resolution rate analysis
